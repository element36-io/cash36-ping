Ping-Pong with Fiat on ethereum

All transactions work within a KYC-ed environment at all times!

Basic template for a Dapp, how to integrate CHF (Swiss Francs) into Smart Contracts. This contracts sends funds from a Person to a contract which can be retrieved later by a defined Person. The wallets must be part of the KYCnetwork.io - transactions and wallets are monitored - its only works, as long participating persons are fully KYC-ed.
